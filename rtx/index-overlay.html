What is Roadtrip Experience Project?

Roadtrip Experience Project was conceived at a creative conference this year in a conversation between an Art Director, Musician & Entrepreneur by the quintessential chai shop.

RTX is a generative travel experiment that brings creatives, influencers & the idea-people together on a Roadtrip to Unlearn, Collaborate & Indulge in Epiphanies..If Being out there is your thing. It seeks to give you self Eureka experience. Let your ID desires roost.

Imagine - ice-peaked mountains, brainstorming in Himalayas, camping by frozen lakes & creative jam sessions by the Ghats bringing back the indelible memories and experience.

Travellers on the Roadtrip come together to create stories, brainstorm on ideas & create beautiful projects on the trip with local entrepreneurs, enculture themselves musicians & artists. These experiences come together to shape the RTX documentary shot on the trip to encourage people to do what they love & living their passions.
