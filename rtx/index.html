What is Road Trip Experience?

A generative travel experiment to unlock your creative awesomeness


Roadtrip Experience Project is once in a lifetime experience for dreamers, doers & ideators. The 9-day unconference on-road brings together creative individuals from various verticals like film, design, startups, music, writing - to fire up their creative intelligence, refresh & collaborate on on-trip projects.

A trip to Indulge in Epiphanies & Self-Eureka Moments travelling through ice-peaked mountains, interacting with amazing people, camping by frozen lakes & being out there in the wild.
