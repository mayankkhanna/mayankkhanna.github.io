About

I am a Software Developer and a self-taught Designer, born and brought up in India in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh. I love brainstorming on new ideas and bringing them to life. I have a strong passion for good design, Design that works functionally and appeals aesthetically..

Currently, I am a graduate student at the School of Information, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, studying Human-Computer interaction. In the recent past, I worked as a Systems Engineer with Infosys Labs, the research wing of Infosys Limited, Bangalore, serving the roles of both a developer and designer. As part of Infosys, I got the opportunity to work on challenging projects and experiment with some really new technologies and in the recent past, worked with their User Experience-Centre of Excellence division.

My research interests are quite varied and revolve around Design, Usability, User Experience and Human-Computer Interaction. In my previous work, I've tried to focus on these areas in fields of Social Networking, Data Visualization and Hybrid-application development. I strongly feel that users should always be delighted by the interfaces they use.
