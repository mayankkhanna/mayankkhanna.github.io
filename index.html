About

A Developer by skill and a Designer by heart, I love brainstorming on new ideas and bringing them to life. I have a strong passion for good design, Design that works functionally and appeals aesthetically.

I have more than four years of experience designing and developing digital interfaces in areas of consumer mobile apps, social networks, Enterprise mobility, Data Visualization and Gamification.

Currently, I design for the next generation of software-defined cloud infrastructure at Cloudistics. It's one of the most complex domains I have ever worked in. Being the second designer on the team, I get to contribute not only to the product but also to the ever-changing design process.

In the recent past, I graduated from the University of Michigan with a Master's in Human-computer interaction. During my master's, I interned as an Interaction designer at Kodak Alaris designing for their flagship mobile app, Kodak Moments. Working at Kodak Alaris exposed me to the design culture of a startup and a re-inventing business.
