About

A Developer by skill and a Designer by heart, I love brainstorming on new ideas and bringing them to life. I have a strong passion for good design, Design that works functionally and appeals aesthetically..

Currently, I am a graduate student at the School of Information, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, studying Human-Computer Interaction & Social Computing with more than three years of professional experience in areas of Social applications, Enterprise mobility, Consumer applications, Data Visualization and Gamification.

In the recent past, I worked as a Senior Systems Engineer with Infosys Labs, the research wing of Infosys Limited, Bangalore, rethinking their client offerings and product strategies. As part of Infosys, I got the opportunity to work on challenging projects, experiment with some really new technologies and work with their User Experience-Centre of Excellence division.

I strongly feel that users should always be delighted by the interfaces they use.
