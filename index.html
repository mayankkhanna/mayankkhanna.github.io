About

I am a Software Developer and a self-taught Designer born and brought up in India in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh and presently residing in the garden city, Bangalore. I love brainstorming on new ideas and bringing them to life. I have a strong passion for good design, Design that works!!

Currently, I work as a Systems Engineer with Infosys Labs, the research wing of Infosys Limited, Bangalore. As part of Infosys, I got the opportunity to work on challenging projects and experiment with some really new technologies. My colleagues usually find me, designing in the code.

My research interests are quite varied and revolve around Design, Usability, User Experience and Human-Computer Interaction. I strongly feel that users should always be delighted by the interfaces they use.

Though I have a background in Softwares, I've recently tried to explore Design and Hardware programming through several meetups and events, in and around Bangalore- DesignDay, IoT-Blr, UXINDIA
