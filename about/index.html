About

A Developer by skill and a Designer by heart, I love brainstorming on new ideas and bringing them to life. I have a strong passion for good design, Design that works functionally and appeals aesthetically.

I have more than three years of designing and developing digital experiences in areas of consumer mobile apps, social networks, Enterprise mobility, Data Visualization and Gamification.

Currently, I am a graduate student at the School of Information, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, studying Human-Computer Interaction & Social Computing and graduating in April, 2016.

In the recent past, I worked as an Interaction design intern at Kodak Alaris designing the future versions of their flagship mobile app, Kodak Moments. Working at Kodak Alaris exposed me to the design culture of a startup and a re-inventing business.
