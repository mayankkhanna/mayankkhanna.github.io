For the love of wireframing

I really enjoy creating quick low-fidelity wireframes around concepts. Some of these pieces are works in progress and some of them were just used to ideate quickly for a concept.
Hope you enjoy seeing these as much as I enjoyed creating them.

Lifesaver

A Gamified iPad application that allows the user to keep track of his savings. Apart from tracking the current savings, it helps him/her to set goals and achieve them.
The application also educates the user on how he should plan his savings based on his existing profile and recommends tenure-based goals based on his current age.

SugarBuddy

An app that assists young children with Diabetes in understanding their health symptoms

Redesigning the Drivewise app

A redesigned gamified version of the Allstate Drivewise app that motivates children to learn driving safely.
