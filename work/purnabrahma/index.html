Purnabramha - Maharastrian Delicacies

Role | Designer - Identity & Branding

Purnabramha is a recently opened restaurant in Bangalore, India that specializes in authentic Marathi Food. I got the opportunity to do the entire branding and design their promotional materials as well which included Business cards, brochures and their menus.

The work I did for them has been a very exciting one as it gave the opportunity to do something at such a large scale and that also independently.
