Gigapixel Imaging

As part of our curriculum, I chose to research on the topic of “Gigapixel Imaging” for my final-year presentation. The topic was something that interested me a lot and was radically different from the typical presentations on several software concepts.

In addition, to the presentation, I also learnt how to use the image stitching software, Autopano. Using this I tried to stitch a few pieces of a part of my college premises that I had specially photographed. I was highly appreciated for my work by my faculty.
